Biggest weakness

The pattern I see is this:

# Analyse

Je : I

ouvre : open

porte : door

That's translation.

Not grammar.

For a tutor I'd rather see

Je — subject pronoun

ouvre — present tense of ouvrir

la — definite article

porte — feminine noun

de — preposition

la maison — noun phrase

This teaches French.

Another thing I'd improve

Your answers always end with

Plus naturel :

Sometimes that's great.

Sometimes there isn't a more natural alternative.

I'd make it conditional.

Only include it if

there's an idiom,
or a common spoken alternative.

Otherwise omit it.
